PROJECTS:

  • Smart Medical Robot for Contactless Priliminary Health Check up of COVID Patients.

The objective of this project is to minimize the contact and the time of interaction between doctors and
patients for preliminary tests which can be conducted autonomously by developing an Autonomous
Smart Medical Assistant Robot for contactless preliminary testing of patients.The project aims to provide
a contactless testing method to the doctors in hospitals to conduct initial health checkups like measuring
the body temperature of the patients, their heart rate, pulse rate and oxygen levels.The robot contains
sensors like Oximeter MAX 30105 to measure the blood oxygen saturation and heart rate of the patients as
a preliminary test criteria.

  • Touchless Doorbell.

Touchless doorBell We have made a small mini project on modernizing of door bells.
In which the user just have to wave his hand in front of the door bells, after getting
sensed by the sensor automatically gets ringed and the occupantsare alerted about the
presence of visitors. Touchless Doorbell is a unique idea and a next step towards safer future.
